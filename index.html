About Me

Turning data into actionable insights and strategies.

Hi, I’m Alan Blbas—a data enthusiast with a knack for uncovering stories hidden in numbers. My journey began in project and event management, where I honed my skills in strategic planning, stakeholder coordination, and delivering results under pressure. Today, I channel that experience into the world of data analytics, helping organizations make smarter, data-driven decisions.

I specialize in Excel, SQL, and Power BI, crafting dashboards and reports that not only look great but also provide actionable insights. Whether it’s optimizing processes, identifying trends, or solving complex problems, I thrive on turning raw data into meaningful strategies that drive growth and efficiency.

Beyond the numbers, I’m passionate about bridging the gap between data and decision-making. I believe that every dataset has a story to tell, and my mission is to bring those stories to life in ways that inspire action. Let’s connect and explore how we can transform your data into a powerful tool for success!
